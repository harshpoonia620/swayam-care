Frequently Asked

Pehle session ke baad pasand na aaye toh? +
Pehle session ke baad agar aap satisfied nahi hain toh hum expert change kar sakte hain. Aapki comfort sabse pehle hai. Registration fee refundable nahi hai lekin session reschedule ya expert change free mein hoga.
Online sessions kaise hote hain? +
Google Meet ya Zoom pe — aapki choice. Link WhatsApp pe bheja jaata hai session se 30 min pehle. Pan India available hai — koi bhi city ho.
50% advance kyun? +
Expert aapke liye slot block karta hai — 50% advance se commitment ensure hota hai dono taraf se. Baaki payment pehle session se pehle. PhonePe, GPay, bank transfer — sab accepted.
Session reschedule ho sakta hai? +
24+ ghante pehle batao — free reschedule. 2-24 ghante mein — ek baar free, phir ₹300 charge. 2 ghante se kam — session forfeit. Aapki convenience ke liye timing flexible rakhi gayi hai.
8 sessions mein result milega kya? +
Pehle mahine mein foundation banta hai — better sleep, less stress, improved flexibility feel honi shuru hogi. Real transformation 90 days mein hoti hai isliye 3-month package recommend karte hain. Gym ek din mein nahi banta — wellness bhi nahi.
Kya main beech mein plan upgrade kar sakta hoon? +
Bilkul — kabhi bhi WhatsApp pe batao. Difference amount pay karke us mahine se upgrade ho jaata hai. Downgrade bhi agले महीने से possible hai.
