Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026  ·  Swayam Care, Gurgaon, India

Your privacy is of the highest importance to Swayam Care. We handle your personal and health information with the utmost discretion and care. This policy explains exactly what we collect, why, and how we protect it.

01

Information We Collect

When you interact with Swayam Care — through our website, WhatsApp, Instagram, or in person — we may collect:

  • Identity Information: Full name, age, gender.
  • Contact Information: Phone number (WhatsApp), email address, residential locality.
  • Professional Information: Occupation, company name (voluntarily provided).
  • Health Information: Current health concerns, medical history, lifestyle habits, wellness goals. This is collected via intake forms and during consultations.
  • Session Data: Notes, progress records, and expert observations from wellness sessions.
  • Payment Information: Transaction records. We do not store card details — payments are processed via UPI or Razorpay.
  • Website Usage: Basic analytics such as pages visited and referral source (collected anonymously via Netlify Analytics, if enabled).

02

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely for:

  • Scheduling and coordinating your wellness sessions with the appropriate expert.
  • Personalizing your program based on your health profile and goals.
  • Sending session reminders, confirmations, and progress updates via WhatsApp.
  • Processing payments and maintaining billing records.
  • Improving our programs based on aggregated, anonymized feedback.
  • Contacting you regarding changes to your program or our services.

We do not use your information for automated profiling, targeted advertising, or any purpose beyond the direct delivery of Swayam Care services.

03

Health Data — Special Protection

Health information is among the most sensitive personal data. All health-related information shared with Swayam Care or any collaborating expert is treated as strictly confidential and will never be shared with any third party without your explicit written consent.

Psychology session content carries the highest level of confidentiality. Our psychologist maintains professional ethical standards which prohibit disclosure of any session content. Session notes, if kept, are accessible only to the treating psychologist.

The only exceptions to health data confidentiality are: (a) where you provide written consent, or (b) where disclosure is required by applicable Indian law (e.g., court order or imminent risk of harm to self or others).

04

Data Sharing

Swayam Care does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We share your data only in the following limited circumstances:

  • With your assigned expert: Only the information necessary for them to deliver your session is shared (name, contact, and relevant health context). Experts are contractually bound to maintain confidentiality.
  • With payment processors: Razorpay receives transaction-related data to process payments, governed by their own privacy policy.
  • With form services: If you submit our website contact form, data is processed via Formspree, governed by their privacy policy.
  • As required by law: If compelled by a valid legal order from a competent Indian court or authority.

05

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored in:

  • Secure Google Drive / Google Sheets (access restricted to Harsh Poonia only).
  • WhatsApp Business — governed by WhatsApp's privacy policy.
  • Physical records, if any, are stored securely and destroyed after 2 years of inactivity.

We take reasonable precautions to protect your data from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. However, no system is 100% secure — if you believe your data has been compromised, please contact us immediately.

06

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request correction of any inaccurate information.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data. We will comply within 30 days, except where retention is required by law or billing purposes.
  • Withdrawal of Consent: You may withdraw consent for us to contact you at any time by sending a WhatsApp message. This does not affect lawfulness of processing before withdrawal.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us via WhatsApp at +91 99536 40487.

07

Cookies & Website Tracking

Our website (swayamcare.netlify.app) does not use tracking cookies or third-party advertising trackers. We may use basic anonymous analytics provided by Netlify to understand traffic volumes. No personally identifiable information is collected through the website itself without your active input via the contact form.

08

Children's Privacy

Swayam Care services are intended for adults aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under 18. If a parent or guardian wishes to enroll a minor in a program, written parental consent is required prior to any session.

09

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any material changes will be communicated to active clients via WhatsApp at least 14 days before taking effect. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will always reflect the most current version.

Continued use of Swayam Care services after a policy update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
